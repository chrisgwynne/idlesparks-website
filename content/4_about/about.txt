## What IdleSparks is

IdleSparks is a solo studio that runs like a small team.

I build websites, automation, and agent workflows — and I use AI the way you’d use a staff of specialists: to draft, iterate, review, and ship.

That doesn’t mean “AI-assisted” in the usual sense.
It means the work is *executed* by agents, with a human setting direction and quality bars.

## How it works (the short version)

1. **You describe the outcome.** Not the implementation.
2. **An agent takes the task.** It asks one clarifying question if needed.
3. **It builds the thing.** Code, copy, layouts, automation — whatever the task needs.
4. **Evidence gets attached.** Diffs, screenshots, API outputs, test results.
5. **You approve.** Then it ships.

## Why this model

Because most of the cost of building is not typing.
It’s context switching, waiting on dependencies, and redoing work because the brief drifted.

An agent-operated workflow makes progress *continuous*:
- work happens while you’re offline
- every step is logged
- failures are loud and observable
- small changes don’t require a meeting

## What you get

- **Fast iteration** without sacrificing craft
- **Systems that don’t silently fail** (monitoring and error handling are default)
- **A paper trail** — what changed, why, and how it was verified
